Recipes Recommender System

A machine learning-based recipe recommendation system using collaborative filtering.

1.User Preferences:

The system uses historical data, such as user ratings and interactions with recipes, to understand individual preferences.

By finding similar users or recipes, it predicts which recipes a user might enjoy.

2.Algorithms:

Collaborative filtering techniques, such as user-user or item-item filtering, identify patterns and similarities in user behavior or recipe attributes.

These algorithms suggest new recipes that align with user tastes based on their preferences and the preferences of similar users.

3.Benefits:

Provides personalized recipe recommendations, making meal planning easier and more enjoyable.

Helps users discover new and exciting recipes they might not have found otherwise.

Increases user engagement by offering tailored suggestions that match their culinary interests.

Air Quality Dashboard

Real-time air quality monitoring dashboard with predictive analytics.

1.Real-Time Monitoring:

The dashboard provides real-time monitoring of air quality by collecting data from various sensors and sources.

It displays current levels of pollutants such as PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, and O3, giving users immediate insights into the air quality in their area.

2.Predictive Analytics:

Utilizes advanced machine learning models to predict future air quality trends based on historical data and environmental factors.

These predictions help users and authorities take proactive measures to mitigate pollution and protect public health.
